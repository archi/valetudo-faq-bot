title: Multifloor Support
short-title: multifloor

text:
Due to technical limitations, Valetudo does not support multiple maps. If you need multiple maps, Valetudo likely won't be an option for you.
The topic has been evaluated and rejected many times already. Even the vendor's own method, with multiple engineers on the project, is still not fit for purpose.
See also https://valetudo.cloud/pages/general/why-not-valetudo.html#no-multi-floormulti-map-support
